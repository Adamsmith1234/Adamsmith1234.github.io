Shindotifications

Shindotifications is a one of kind notification system so that you never miss a spawn!
Get notified 5 minutes before each in-game spawn! This feature is progressively being expanded!
To get the full functionality of Shindotifications...

  • Press the button above to activate it!
  • Resize this window to your liking, just don't minimize it!
  • Ensure that your roblox game is not in fullscreen mode!
    You can do this by simply pressing "Shift" and "F11"
  • Vesion 1.1.0: Works for all spawns as of 12/14/20.
